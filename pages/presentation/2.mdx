## **Recommended Reading List**

### **1. Blockchain and Smart Contract Auditing**
- **[ZeppelinOS: Operational System for Smart Contracts](https://openzeppelin.com/)** – This paper discusses a framework for managing the lifecycle of smart contracts, with a focus on upgradeability and security, which is crucial for auditing processes.
- **[A Survey of Smart Contract Formal Verification Techniques](https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.10337)** – This paper offers an overview of the various methods used to formally verify smart contracts, an important aspect of ensuring their security.
- **[Finding The Greedy, Prodigal, and Suicidal Contracts at Scale](https://arxiv.org/abs/1802.06038)** – Focuses on the detection of vulnerabilities in Ethereum smart contracts, which directly relates to Mamori's goal of identifying economic exploits.

### **2. Convex Optimization (Review)**
- **[Convex Optimization by Stephen Boyd and Lieven Vandenberghe](https://web.stanford.edu/~boyd/cvxbook/)** – A foundational text that covers the principles of convex optimization, which underpins the convex flow problem and related optimization techniques.
- **[Duality in Optimization](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/mathematics/18-06-linear-algebra-spring-2010/readings/)** – Understanding the concept of duality is key for grasping how problems can be transformed from maximization to minimization, as in the convex flow problem.
- **[Fenchel Conjugates and Duality in Convex Optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/1104.0970)** – This paper provides an in-depth exploration of Fenchel conjugates, which are crucial in formulating the dual problem from the primal one.

### **3. Algorithm Design for Optimization**
- **[The BFGS Method for Large-Scale Optimization](https://epubs.siam.org/doi/10.1137/1.9781611975147)** – A detailed look at the BFGS quasi-Newton method, which is used in solving the dual problem efficiently in large-scale networks.
- **[Numerical Optimization by Jorge Nocedal and Stephen Wright](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-0-387-40065-5)** – A comprehensive guide to optimization algorithms, including methods like BFGS, which are key to solving convex optimization problems.

### **4. Blockchain Performance and Scalability**
- **[Ethereum: A Secure Decentralized Generalized Transaction Ledger](https://ethereum.org/en/whitepaper/)** by Vitalik Buterin – Essential reading for understanding the underlying technology behind smart contracts.
- **[Blockchain Scalability and Its Foundations in Distributed Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/1809.03421)** – This paper examines the challenges of scaling blockchain systems, which is relevant for optimizing performance in smart contract auditing.

### **5. Network Flow Optimization**
- **[Network Flows: Theory, Algorithms, and Applications](https://www.amazon.com/Network-Flows-Theory-Algorithms-Applications/dp/013617549X)** by Ravindra K. Ahuja, Thomas L. Magnanti, and James B. Orlin – A comprehensive guide to network flow problems, offering insights into the types of challenges solved by convex flow optimization.
- **[A Fast Parallel Algorithm for the Max-Flow Problem](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/800076.802479)** – This paper explores parallel computing approaches to solving flow problems, which is relevant given Mamori's need for scalable solutions.
